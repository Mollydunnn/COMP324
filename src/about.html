What is Thread Core?

Thread Core is a high-end ecommerce space for individual artists or brands to showcase their products to a larger audience that they cannot reach on their own. We serve as a marketplace for customers to access individual artists and smaller brands all in one place. This application gives subscribers early access to exclusive drops, and the ability to keep up with sought after and trending fashion.
